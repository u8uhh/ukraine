About Should/Shouldn't

We use should when we need to do something, when we use this verb we never put after this portion "to",let's go to give en example:He should do sport. Shouldn't we use when we say about thinks which people don't need, let's give an example: he shouldn't play games a lot. Of course to this topic fits special verb "must/mustn't",this verb same as verb "should". Example with verb must and mustn't: I must go home,He mustn't do bad thinks.
